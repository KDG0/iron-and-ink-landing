Our Story

Crafted with intention,
worn with pride

Iron & Ink was born from a simple belief: tattoos aren't just ink on skin — they're stories etched into the body. Founded in the heart of Williamsburg, Brooklyn, our studio brings together a collective of artists who treat every piece as a collaboration between artist and canvas. We don't do walk-ins, we don't rush, and we never compromise. Every session begins with a conversation.

What We Do

Styles & Services

Every style demands a different discipline. Our artists specialize across traditions — from the precision of fine line work to the boldness of Japanese heritage.

Fine Line

Delicate, precise linework for minimalist designs, botanicals, micro-realism, and intricate geometric patterns.

$150+ starting

Japanese Traditional

Irezumi-inspired work honoring centuries of tradition — koi, dragons, waves, and full compositional sleeves.

$200+ starting

Neo-Traditional

Bold outlines meet modern illustration — vibrant color palettes, dynamic compositions, and expressive forms.

$180+ starting

Blackwork

Striking black ink compositions — dotwork, geometric patterns, ornamental designs, and heavy coverage pieces.

$120+ starting
